The $200 Billion Problem

Despite ITSM maturity, enterprises lose millions annually due to repeat incidents, tribal knowledge, and ineffective knowledge reuse. Incident management remains the #1 driver of IT operations cost, with most organizations repeatedly solving the same problems.

13% of incidents are repeat or known issues
20-30% of resolution time lost to poor knowledge reuse
25-40% knowledge loss from employee turnover

Traditional ITSM Limitations

  • • Manual known error discovery
  • • No workflow-driven governance
  • • No duplicate/stale detection
  • • No effectiveness measurement
  • • Limited enterprise auditability
